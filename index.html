StatClub @ Southern Methodist University

The "StatClub" is an undergraduate statistics club at Southern Methodist University. It is a student-run academic group with the goal of providing students with tools and opportunities to grow within the field of statistics. We hold events throughout the year.

For more information, please contact our president: Hannah Cramer (email address).
